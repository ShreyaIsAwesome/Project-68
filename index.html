Dog Stages

Adolescence

After 5-6 months of being a Puppy it turns into a Adolescence. Here are some fun facts about Adolescence!
Adolescents are highly sensitive to respect
Teething is almost done!
Teenage dogs become more interested in going for walks

Adulthood

After 1 year of being a Puppy it goes in it's Adulthood Stage. Here are some fun facts about Adulthood Dogs!
Dogs can sniff at the same time as breathing
Their sense of smell is at least 40x better than ours
Some have such good noses they can sniff out medical problems
Now Dogs can get puppies and the life cycle begins again!
